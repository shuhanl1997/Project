I am Shuhan Liu and I am a third year SIAT major student based in Simon Fraser University. A Chinese girl having grown up in a traditional family. I love arts and culture, and I exploited many classical elements for my own design. My keen passion in design since my childhood has filled my life with more inspirations. I was exposed to knowledge of interactive design and graphic design. I fairly enjoyed the group works, as I realized that opinions and suggestions from others are conducive to addressing my own deficiencies and to creating diversity and possibility in our design work.
